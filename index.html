About Us

What is Khadhya
Khurak?

•  It is a prestigious Food and Hospitality event curated to showcase the current trends and technological advancements in the Food and Hospitality industry across India.
• Khadhya Khurak has gained immense success for more than a decade now and has some remarkable stats: The exhibition has witnessed a footfall of more than a million attendees since its inception 18 years ago.
• 5000+ brands showcase their products and services.
• Has found support and patronage from reputed regulatory authorities like FSSAI and Government of Gujarat.

Well Wishes For Nobel Cause

Shree Bhupendrabhai Patel Hon'ble CM-(Gujarat State) Dt.04/12/2023
  • CM Greetings!

In the modern era, alongside physicians, doctors are also advocating for the consumption of grains and vegetables according to seasonal cycles. The introduction of 'Millets,' long recognized in India as nutritious grains, was made known to the world by our successful Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi a year ago. These grains, devoid of fat, are rich in nutritional elements. Creating a diet in line with the nutritional qualities of our traditional grains is a progressive step.

Inspired by the honorable Prime Minister, the International Food Expo, organized for the last 19 years, has become a center of attraction for citizens. Learning about the Food Expo 2023, to be held in Gandhinagar from December 17th to 20th, brought me immense joy. This expo not only generates employment but also serves as an encouragement for entrepreneurs involved in Gujarat's and India's food industries, alongside spreading new energy among food enthusiasts. I believe that the exhibition will continue to be encouraging for all stakeholders in providing a successful source of benefits for everyone's well-being. Best wishes for the success of the exhibition, serving the interests of all.

(Bhupendra Patel)
